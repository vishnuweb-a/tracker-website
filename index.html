The StudySprint Methodology

A proven cycle to build consistency and achieve your exam goals.

Step 01

Create a Sprint

Define a focused period (e.g., 7 days, 14 days) to tackle specific chapters or subjects without burning out.

Step 02

Log Daily Entries

Every day, log your study time, revision time, questions solved per subject, and brief daily notes.

Step 03

Build Habits

Check off your daily habits like solving PYQs, revising formulas, or completing lectures to maintain momentum.

Step 04

Analyze Progress

Review the visual analytics dashboard to see your performance score, weekly trends, and identify weak spots.

Step 05

Complete & Repeat

Finish the sprint, review the historical summary, and start a new sprint armed with better insights about yourself.
